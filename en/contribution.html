Isamu Defense Fund

History

As soon as Isamu Kaneko arrested, many people are willing to support him. Especially they want to fund him for defense. They are engineers, and non-engineers who seriously concern for freedom status in Japan.

On May 11th, we started to accept a bank transfer for support him. We raised an enormous 10million yen ($100,000) in 10days. This amount of money shows how many people are really concerning about the issue.

We are now accepting a donation via Paypal. Please send to contact@freekaneko.com, or click the button below. Thank you for your support!

We respect your privacy. Doners' name, e-mail, and any other information will be kept confidential. Only a date and amount may be displayed. And we don't use for any other purpose. Regretfully, a donation is not tax deductable under Japanese law, even extra tax (a donation tax) may be charged to donee...
